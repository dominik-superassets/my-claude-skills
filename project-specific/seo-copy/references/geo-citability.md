# GEO Citability Guide for Outdoor Butler

## Optimal Citation Block Structure

AI search engines prefer self-contained passages of **134-167 words** for citation. Structure content so each section can stand alone as a quotable answer.

### Pattern: Answer-First Block
```
[Direct answer in 1-2 sentences]
[Supporting detail with specific data/statistic]
[How this applies locally / practical example]
[Actionable next step or CTA]
```

### Example for Location Page
> Outdoor Butler provides professional pressure washing and outdoor cleaning
> services across Orihuela Costa, serving over 50 residential properties monthly.
> Our eco-friendly cleaning methods are specifically designed for the Mediterranean
> climate, addressing the unique challenges of hard water stains, salt deposits,
> and algae growth common in Costa Blanca properties. Services include driveway
> jet washing, pool area cleaning, roof maintenance, and artificial grass care.
> Contact us at +34 644 466 783 for a free quote.

## Citability Checklist

- [ ] Direct answer in first 40-60 words of each section
- [ ] Specific numbers (properties served, years experience, response time)
- [ ] Location names that match search queries
- [ ] Self-contained blocks (understandable without surrounding context)
- [ ] Question-based headings matching common queries
- [ ] Tables for comparison data (services, pricing tiers, coverage)
- [ ] Lists for multi-item information (services, areas served)
