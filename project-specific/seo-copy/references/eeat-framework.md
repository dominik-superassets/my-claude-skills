# E-E-A-T Framework for Outdoor Butler

E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is Google's content quality framework, updated in the September 2025 Quality Rater Guidelines.

## Experience (First-Hand Signals)

For a local service business like Outdoor Butler, experience signals include:
- Before/after photos of actual cleaning jobs
- Case studies from real properties in the service area
- Process documentation (how we clean stamped concrete, etc.)
- Unique local insights (climate effects on Spanish properties)
- Customer testimonials with specific details

### How to incorporate
- Reference specific local conditions: "The Mediterranean climate means..."
- Mention specific materials common in the area: "Stamped concrete driveways common in Costa Blanca urbanizations..."
- Include process details that show hands-on knowledge

## Expertise

- Technical cleaning knowledge (PSI settings, chemicals, techniques)
- Material-specific expertise (different surfaces need different approaches)
- Equipment knowledge (professional vs consumer grade)
- Safety protocols (eco-friendly products, surface protection)

### How to incorporate
- Use accurate technical terminology naturally
- Explain why certain methods work for specific surfaces
- Reference industry standards and best practices

## Authoritativeness

- Consistent brand presence across Google Business Profile, social media
- Citations from local directories and review sites
- Published content demonstrating deep domain knowledge
- Coverage of comprehensive service area (14 location pages)

### How to incorporate
- Cross-link between related service and location pages
- Reference specific service areas by name
- Maintain consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all pages

## Trustworthiness

- Clear contact information on every page (+34 644 466 783)
- Physical service area clearly defined
- HTTPS, privacy policy, terms of service
- Real customer reviews and ratings
- Transparent pricing page
- Professional, error-free content (no typos in brand name)

### How to incorporate
- Always include contact info and CTA
- Keep content accurate and up-to-date
- Fix all typos immediately (damages trust in search results)

## AI Content Assessment (Sep 2025 QRG)

Google raters now formally assess AI-generated content. To pass:
- Demonstrate genuine E-E-A-T (local experience, specific expertise)
- Provide unique value (not generic cleaning advice)
- Show human oversight and local knowledge
- Include original insights specific to the service area
- Avoid generic phrasing and repetitive structure
