## AI Crawler Access

Ensure robots.txt allows: GPTBot, OAI-SearchBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot.
Consider adding `/llms.txt` with structured site description.

## Quick Wins for Local Service Business

1. Answer-first format: direct answer in first 40-60 words of each section
2. Self-contained answer blocks of 134-167 words (optimal citation length)
3. Question-based H2/H3 headings matching search queries
4. Include phone number (+34 644 466 783) and service area in citeable format
5. Tables comparing services, pricing tiers, or coverage areas
6. Clear location references: "Orihuela Costa", "Alicante province", "Costa Blanca"
